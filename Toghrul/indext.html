My Interests

Since my childhood I have been interested in technology. My parents tell me I left no toys intact. I used to disassemble them, try to understand why when I press a button, the car moves. I didn't only disassemble. I've always tried to fix broken things around. Of course, many times without success:-)

Although significant part of students considers subjects like Math, Physics and Chemistry dull or even hate them, I've always found and still find them attractive and breathtaking. For me there is no better knowledge than the one that explains underlaying processes for everything that we experience in daily life.

I do not think I am big at design and aesthetics. Yet, seeing all sorts of content and all sorts of design published on Youtube by all sorts of Youtube creators, which I should confess tempt me, I've decided not to put out there anything unpolished and amateur. Finding the perfect balance between science, design and art is my target.
